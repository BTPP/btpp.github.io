INSTRUMENTAL DIVERSITY

A Musical instrument is a device which produces music or a sound with the help of some human efforts. Any material or device which produces music is termed as a musical instrument. The academic study of musical instruments is called organology. Here are some of the popular instruments.

INDIAN INSTRUMENTS

  • SITAR AND SAROD

  • SARANGI, TANPURA AND ESRAJ

  • SANTOOR, VICHITRA VEENA AND VIOLIN

  • TABLA, PAKHAWAJ AND MRIDANGAM

  • DHOLAK, JAL TARANG AND PUNG

  • FLUTE , SHEHNAI AND OTHER INSTRUMENTS
