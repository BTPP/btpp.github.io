GOSPEL ,FLUTE AND MEDITATION MUSIC

GOSPEL MUSIC

Gospel music is that which is written in order to express emotions regarding the Christian faith. It has beautiful melodies and rhythm. Read on to know all about it.

 

FLUTE MUSIC

Flute music is the music produced by playing a flute. Flute is a musical instrument from the wood wind group. Flute music is created by passing air across an opening or embouchure.

 

MEDITATION MUSIC

Meditation Music is a type of silent music which is especially played or listened during meditation. It requires extreme silence and is part of the branch of yoga called as 'Nada Yoga'. Read on the following articles to learn how this music is played or listened.
